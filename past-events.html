Jumping Drill Team 2014-19

The Colchester Ag Society sponsored the Jumping Drill Team that performed at the Saturday night Extravaganza at the Mane Event Horse Expo in Red Deer from 2014-2019. They also performed at the SAHA Horse Showcase at Fultonvale and Vegreville Chuckwagon Races in 2018.
