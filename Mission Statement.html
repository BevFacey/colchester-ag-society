Colchester & District Agricultural Society

For residents that live in or around the Strathcona County area in Alberta, Canada.

Mission Statement & Vision

Mission Statement “To engage the community in activities and learning experiences that promote the importance of agriculture. To provide educational opportunities related to agriculture, particularly dealing with food production from small land holdings.”

Vision “To promote agriculture in Strathcona County while recognizing that the area we are located in is no longer principally agricultural but is a mix of smaller holdings and country residential. To provide financial aid to local non profit organizations To provide scholarships at agricultural colleges To provide funds to local schools for farming related activities To promote 4H within Strathcona County and surrounding districts”
